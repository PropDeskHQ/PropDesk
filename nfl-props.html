How It Works

PropDesk pulls live odds from many US sportsbooks and scores each prop on value, not gut feel.

1. Line Collection

We query The Odds API for every available sportsbook line on a given player prop market. For each game and market we gather the line (for example 24.5 yards) and the American odds on both sides from every book that posts it.

2. Consensus Line

The consensus is the median line across all books. The median is more robust than an average because it ignores one outlier book that may be slow to move. This consensus becomes the reference point for measuring edge.

3. Best Bet

For Over/Under markets we pick the side that gives you the most free points versus consensus. If one book hangs 23.5 while everyone else is at 24.5, the Under at 24.5 or the Over at 23.5 carries real edge. Ties break to the best odds. For Yes/No markets such as anytime TD we simply find the best price.

4. Value Score

Every prop gets a 0-100 score built from line-shopping edge, odds quality, sportsbook vig, number of books posting, and how tightly books agree. Higher means more demonstrable edge.

TierScoreMeaning
HOT80-100Strong, well-supported edge
GOOD65-79Solid value worth a play
FAIR48-64Marginal lean
WEAK32-47Thin, proceed with caution
PASS0-31No meaningful edge

5. Player Stats

We pull season averages, recent game logs, and over-rate from official league sources (MLB Stats API) and ESPN (NFL, NBA, NHL) so you can sanity-check a line against actual production.

6. Parlay Builder

The builder assembles the best individual legs into a combined ticket and shows true combined odds and payout. Lay of the Day caps total odds near +500 and limits legs to keep correlation and variance reasonable.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not predict outcomes or guarantee wins.
  • We do not place bets for you.
  • We do not sell picks. The analysis is computed locally in your browser.
  • We do not store your data on a server. Your API key and history live in your browser only.
